Our Mission at charity: water

At charity: water, our mission is to bring clean, safe drinking water to communities in need around the world. We prioritize 100% transparency: every donation you make directly funds water projects, tracked via GPS, and accompanied by regular updates so you can see exactly where and how your gift is making an impact.
